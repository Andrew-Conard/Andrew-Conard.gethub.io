Data Visualization using Tableau

In a recent personal project, I delved into a comprehensive dataset of British Airways' customer reviews, spanning a range of aircraft and seating classes. Utilizing Tableau, I meticulously analyzed and visualized the data to uncover insights into passenger experiences. My analysis focused on various aspects of air travel, including cabin staff service, in-flight entertainment, food and beverage quality, ground service efficiency, overall passenger satisfaction ratings, seat comfort, and the perceived value for money. This project not only demonstrated my ability to handle and interpret complex datasets but also showcased my expertise in utilizing Tableau to bring data-driven stories to life. The findings offer valuable perspectives for both consumers making travel decisions and airlines aiming to enhance service quality.
